Instructions

Click to select 3 cards that have properties that are either all the same or all different. The 4 properties are:

  1. Color
  2. Number (of symbols)
  3. Shape
  4. Shading

If your selection is not a valid set, an explaination will appear to the right of the game area. Click selected cards to deselect them.
